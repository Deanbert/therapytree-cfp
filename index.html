What is Rapid Transformational Therapy (RTT)?

RTT is an award winning therapy that combines the best principles of:

  • Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT)
  • Neuro Linguistic Programming (NLP)
  • Psycotherapy
  • Hypnosis

RTT concentrates on helping you understand the root of your issue in the shortest possible time.
