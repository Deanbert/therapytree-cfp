    Check out my Happy Clients section, I think what they have to say speaks volumes to the power of this therapy!

  • How Rapid is Rapid Transformational Therapy?

    Every client experiences a varied speed of change. Really, it all depends on the customer. The best course of action is to embrace your own experience and avoid comparisons. Despite this, many people do experience change and start a shift within 30 days.

  • What Principles Do You Use for Clients?

    I have many! It is a passion full of lifelong learning. Although if a client is hesitant about hypnosis, I may not use it in some circumstances. The advantage of having training in a variety of theraputic practices is that it allows me to adjust as necessary. I integrate my knowledge from a variety of practices which can also include Reiki and Yoga. This is because we quickly get to the heart of the matter and reframe beliefs to make breakthroughs that would not be feasible with conscious self-talk.

  • Will I Get Better Results from an Online or in Person Session?

    The simple answer is no, and here's why I think online sessions are more efficient: Your mind's purpose is to keep you safe; it enjoys the familiar, so when you enter a strange clinic, your mind goes into immediate alert mode and the "White Coat Syndrome" kicks in and causes you to feel uneasy. This has an effect on the session and how which a client may open up.

  • How Will I Feel After Just One Session?

    My sessions are really intense. I'm there to help you achieve the greatest outcome possible. Some clients claim to feel emotional, worn out, and that they simply want to roll over and sleep after the session. The beautiful thing about online sessions is that you can do it from the comfort of your own bed.

  • Why Should I Choose You?

    Finding a therapist who is right for you, who understands you, with whom you click, and who you sense is committed to your path is crucial. I take pride in this. All I want for my customers is to regain the self-confidence I once lost and use purpose to bring happiness into their lives. Every day, the work I perform with clients makes me happy. When I am with my clients, even on difficult days or when life has dealt me a curve ball, I am content. I am extremely appreciative of the enthusiasm my clients offer me. That being said, if after one or two sessions you feel we have not connected I will happily recommend you to another highly reputable therapist.
